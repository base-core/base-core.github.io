TOKEN PURCHASE PROCESS

Register with a distributor or an exchange

Buy Pass tokens from an exchange or distributor. Note that PASS tokens are the 'fuel' of the Blockpass ecosystem and are used ONLY by businesses and merchants aiming to save on their KYC

Go to one of the official PASS token distributors or an go to an official exchange

Activate your PASS Tokens

Once you have received your tokens, click the activation link inside the Blockpass app and accept the protocol terms. We will verify your identity and when finished, your tokens will become transferable. The PASS Activation Service is now available in Blockpass mobile application. Read the Step-By-Step User Guide to Activating PASS tokensOr connect the PASS Activation Service on the web.

Choose your wallet

You can use any ERC-20 compatible wallet to interact with your PASS tokens. We have a partnership with Infinito Wallet that natively support our token.
